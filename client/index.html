      What is a Travel Buddy?

      More than just splitting costs—it's about sharing the journey.

      A Travel Buddy is a like-minded explorer going your way. It’s not just about filling an empty seat; it's about connecting with verified students who share your destination and vibe. Whether you need someone to navigate with or just feel safer traveling together, the right companion transforms a trip into an experience.

      • Enhanced Safety: Travel with confidence knowing you aren't alone in a new place.
      • Smart Savings: Split cabs, autos, and travel costs effortlessly.
      • Build Connections: Turn a boring commute into an opportunity to make new friends.
